Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, appointments, and facilities.

How do I schedule an appointment with a doctor?

You can book an appointment by calling our reception, using our online form, or visiting us directly. Walk-ins are also welcome depending on availability.

Do you accept health insurance plans?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card during your visit for verification.

What should I bring for my first consultation?

Bring your ID, medical records (if any), current medications, and insurance details. This helps us provide the best possible care from the start.

Do you offer emergency medical services?

We are not an emergency center. In case of life-threatening situations, please visit the nearest hospital emergency room or dial your local emergency number.

Can I consult a specialist directly without a referral?

Yes, you can directly book a consultation with any of our specialists. However, some insurance plans may require a referral for coverage.

How can I access my lab test results?

Lab results can be accessed through our secure patient portal or collected in person. We'll also notify you once your results are ready.
